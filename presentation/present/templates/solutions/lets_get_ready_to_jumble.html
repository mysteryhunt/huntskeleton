Reading the highlighted squares with the wrestlers filled in yields the clue phrase TAPE YOUR HEEL TURN.

In pro wrestling (or TV tropes), a “heel turn” is when a character changes from good to bad. Teams were required to record and send in a heel turn. This may be a short vignette or possibly a dance move. Once they provide a recording, they are provided with this PDF. On the March 13th, 2003 episode of Smackdown, the impostor who was F5'd was ERIC ANGLE, which is the final answer.
