Each of the audio files in this puzzle is a mashup representing an official drink used by the International Bartenders Association (as suggested by Ismene, Becca, and Akira in the flavor text) in the World Cocktail Competition. Each of the songs used in the mashups has lyrics or a title that reference an ingredient used in cocktails.

Each audio clip is a combination of several ingredients (approximately) in proportion according to the IBA guidelines, creating a “sonic cocktail.” For example, 9 parts “Tequila” by The Clash, 18 parts “It Never Crossed My Mind” by Frank Sinatra, and 3 parts “Bel Air” by Lana Del Ray represents a Tequila Sunrise
