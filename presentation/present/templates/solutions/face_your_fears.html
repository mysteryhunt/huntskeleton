Solvers are presented with 10 video clips from a number of animated films. Each film has a section or sections blacked out, with screaming overlaid. Each blacked out section corresponds to the appearance on screen (or the explicit verbal mention) of a particular terribly frightening thing. The movie clips in order and their corresponding blacked-out scary things are:

Movie Scary Thing Video
Osmosis Jones Cartoon-logic X-ray scanner
Shrek the Third Shrek’s baby projectile vomiting
Up Carl’s balloons lifting the house
The Emperor’s New Groove Fly being trapped in a web and eaten by a spider
The Princess and the Frog The frog prince
Fantasia: Night on Bald Mountain Chernabog the demon opening his wings
Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs Tornado made of pasta
Aladdin Aladdin wishes for Genie’s freedom
Zootopia The train Judy takes into the city
The Lion King Pumbaa eating a worm

Each of the 10 scary things corresponds to a specific phobia (clued in the flavortext via “My psychiatrist says I have problems”) which corresponds to the length below each video:

Scary thing Phobia
X-ray RADIOPHOBIA
Vomiting EMETOPHOBIA
Balloons GLOBOPHOBIA
Spiders ARACHNOPHOBIA
Frogs RANIDOPHOBIA
Demons DAEMONOPHOBIA
Tornadoes (or hurricanes) LILAPSOPHOBIA
Freedom ELEUTHEROPHOBIA
Trains SIDERODROMOPHOBIA
Worms SCOLECIPHOBIA

Reading the first letter of each phobia yields the answer, REGARDLESS (or, alternatively, REGARD LESS, a potential strategy for dealing with scary things in a movie).
