As hinted by 5S (“STRIPES”), we are playing a game of 8-ball and we are stripes, which means we simply need to pocket the 8 to win. As usual, we cannot hit an opponent’s ball first. The shortest path is to play the ball to the northeast, which results in hitting 11 cushions and pocketing the 8 ball in the southwest corner. The letters hit along each cushion, in path order, spell POCKET MONEY.
