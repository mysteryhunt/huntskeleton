Once both colors are identified, consider the colors around the hex itself. The colors are Red, Green, and Blue. For each hex, if you subtract the corresponding component (r, g, or b) values of the original from the replaced color, you will get two positive values from 1 through 26. These can be converted to A-Z bigrams.
