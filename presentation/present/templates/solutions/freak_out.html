The audio tracks consist of vowel sounds—mostly screaming. Each is a clip from a song whose title has something to do with fear, and that has been in the Billboard Hot 100 at some point. The comments provide additional clues towards the identities of the artists involved, and the date gives the week that the song hit its peak on the Billboard Hot 100 weekly chart.
