The first score corresponds to the row and the second to the column, and Xs are for matches that haven’t yet been played.

Taking the goals scored in the highest scoring game for each team and indexing into their name as instructed gives the phrase SITE TEAMS ON CLOCK FACE FIRST TEAM USES THE START TIME NOW DRAW REMAINING GAMES. This suggests a way to represent the round robin tournaments geometrically: place the number 1 team at 12 or 1 o’clock on a circle and proceed clockwise, putting team number 6 at 10 or 11 o’clock.
