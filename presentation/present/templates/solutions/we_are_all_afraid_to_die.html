Reading the boxed letters in order gives the clue phrase PLEASE STATE THE NATURE OF THE. Some searching reveals that this is a frequent catchphrase of the Emergency Medical Hologram from Star Trek: Voyager. The answer is the completion of the quote, PLEASE STATE THE NATURE OF THE MEDICAL EMERGENCY.
