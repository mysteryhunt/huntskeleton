This puzzle involves playing through a rogue-like game in the browser. Your character is represented by an “@” symbol and the only controls are the 4 arrow keys (no diagonal movement). The goal on each level is to make it to the down staircase (represented by a “>”).

There are 22 levels. After solving a level you are taken to a new unique URL for the next level. These URLs can be shared with teammates so that they can immediately jump to the same point of progress.

Along the way you will encounter several different monsters and obstacles.

At roughly the halfway point through the levels, a new mechanic is introduced where there is a second character “@”. The movement keys move both @ characters at the same time. The goal is to keep both @ characters alive and take them both to the 2 down staircases (they must both be on the staircases at the same time).

In the final level there is no down staircase. Instead there is an " symbol which represents the amulet, and a collection of monsters represented by lower case letters. After making your way to the amulet the monster letters will spell out the final answer, KEYBOARD PLAYER.
