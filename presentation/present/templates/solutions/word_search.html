Looking at the word search table, one might notice that the letter frequencies are off, and overall it looks encrypted. We try a substitution cypher. A good place to start is the word PESSIMIST, as the string XXY?YX can be found only in one place. After we place PESSIMIST, it is easy to place STRESS, LONELINESS, and APOCALYPSE.
