This puzzle is a pretty straightforward Konundrum-style puzzle with four players. Follow the rules and write things down… and you’ll get about four steps in.

This is because all the players are pigs, and naturally, they are writing in the Pigpen cipher! This means that letters will look different to different players, as well as in different orderings. (Before the game begins, this fact is hinted by “ham-handed” in the flavortext as well as the names of the pigs.)

As you follow the rules, it should be mostly straightforward, but there are a few dirty tricks:

  • Cicero’s Wrath Breakdown happens during another player’s turn.
  • There is one instance in the instructions where a player is instructed to “dot an I”—that turns it into an R!
  • When the game ends, Cicero flips the table. The table is glass, and therefore you can see through it—which makes the letters look different!

Following all of the instructions and reading the letters from the flipped table will give you the final instruction: PLEASE OINK FOR HEADQUARTERS. When this task is completed, solvers will be given the final answer, {{ puzzle.puzzleProperties.AnswersProperty.answers.0.canonicalAnswer }}.
